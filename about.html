About Me

Currently pursuing my MA in Graphic and Web Design at Minneapolis College of Art and Design, I bring a unique perspective to UX/UI design through my technical foundation in software quality assurance.

My hybrid background means I understand both sides of designer-developer collaboration. I know how developers read documentation, what edge cases to anticipate, and how to create specifications that eliminate ambiguity. This makes me naturally detail-oriented about accessibility compliance, pixel-perfect execution, and quality throughout the design process.

What excites me most is creating inclusive digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. At ClockShark, I reduced defect rates by 50% through improved collaboration and I bring this same quality-focused, collaborative approach to design work.

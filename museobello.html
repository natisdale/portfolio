Museo Bello

The goal of the Museo Bello app, is to to provide patrons and contributing artists with access to information about exhibits, events, membership and calls for art.

User Journey

Walking through actions provided insights into opportunities for new functionality as well as improvements in experience.

Sketches

A variety of designs for each page in the app were first sketched out on paper.

Then a composite wireframe was made based on the best features of each design.

Mockups

Original landing page was the Events list, but based on the Usability Study with the low-fidelity prototype, created a new home page
